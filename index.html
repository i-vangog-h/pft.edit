Project presentation

Idea

This video project is a fan video edit for one the Practal Fraktal songs. The project aimes to illustrate the core concept and general vibe of the band's music, as well as to capture very important and, by now already, nostalgic personally for me, the author, moments of the early days, when this video was filmed [January 2023].


Filming

All photo and video content for this video was filmed on a
Canon IXUS 40 camera. Audio used is mostly from the camera shots, Iphone Voice Memos was utilized to record some of the voiceovers.


Editing

Photo, video and audio content was processed and edited using Adobe Premiere Pro software. Editing process included basic video editing and composition, application of effects such as color correction, color inversion, video and audio transions (fade in/out), application of audio envelopes, audio mixing, and audio equalization and noise reduction.
